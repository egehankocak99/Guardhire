Senior Data Engineer
Vault Payments Ltd — London, UK (Hybrid)

ABOUT VAULT PAYMENTS
Vault Payments is a regulated fintech building next-generation payment
infrastructure for European banks and digital wallet providers. We process
over 5 billion transactions annually across 18 markets. Our engineering
teams operate at the intersection of financial systems, real-time data,
and machine learning. We are regulated by the FCA and operate under PSD2.

THE ROLE
We are looking for a Senior Data Engineer to join our Core Data Platform
team. You will own critical components of our real-time data infrastructure,
working closely with data scientists, ML engineers, and product teams to
build reliable, scalable, and observable data systems. This is a technical
individual contributor role with significant scope for growth into a staff
or principal engineering track.

CORE RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Design, build, and operate streaming and batch data pipelines at scale
    (5B+ events/day) using Apache Kafka and Apache Spark.
  • Own the reliability of our AWS-based data lake and warehouse (S3,
    Glue Catalog, Redshift, dbt transformation layer).
  • Implement and maintain data quality frameworks and SLAs across
    production pipelines serving ML feature stores and BI dashboards.
  • Lead architectural decisions for our transition from batch to
    streaming-first data processing.
  • Collaborate with the ML Platform team to engineer production-grade
    feature pipelines for fraud detection and credit risk models.
  • Champion DataOps practices: CI/CD for data pipelines, automated
    testing, infrastructure-as-code (Terraform).
  • Participate in on-call rotation for production data platform incidents.

ESSENTIAL REQUIREMENTS
  • 5+ years of professional experience as a Data Engineer.
  • Expert-level proficiency in Python and SQL.
  • Strong hands-on experience with Apache Kafka and Apache Spark
    (Structured Streaming or Flink).
  • Practical experience with AWS data services: S3, Glue, Redshift or
    equivalent cloud data warehouse.
  • Experience with dbt for transformation layer development and testing.
  • Proficiency with Apache Airflow or an equivalent orchestration tool.
  • Solid understanding of distributed systems and streaming architectures.
  • Experience with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform or CloudFormation).

DESIRABLE SKILLS
  • Experience in regulated financial services (FCA, PSD2, GDPR context).
  • Familiarity with real-time fraud detection or risk scoring systems.
  • Scala or Java experience for JVM-based Spark/Flink optimisation.
  • Contributions to open-source data engineering projects.
  • Experience with Databricks or Snowflake.
  • Knowledge of data mesh or data product architectural patterns.

WHAT WE OFFER

  • Hybrid working: 2 days/week in our Shoreditch office.
  • 30 days annual leave + bank holidays.
  • Private healthcare, dental, and life insurance.
  • £2,000 annual learning & development budget.
  • Employee share options scheme.
  • Sponsorship for professional certifications (AWS, Databricks, etc.).
